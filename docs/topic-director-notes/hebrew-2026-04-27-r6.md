## 2. Calibration assessment

### Anti-pattern #1 evaluation (target revision)

r5's targets (≥1,500 verb count; <10% None-grammar) were aspirational, not source-evidenced. Per anti-pattern #1, Director must not accept revised targets on Builder's word alone — must evaluate whether binary evidence justifies each revision independently.

**Verb count target: ≥1,500 → ≥1,450**

Evidence presented:
- Builder binary scan: 1,453 entries contain HALOT binyan markers
- Verifier-4 independent count: 1,458 entries with non-empty binyanim
- Two independent measurements converge at ~1,453–1,458 as the hard ceiling
- The 13 entries above the 1,453 floor (reaching 1,471) are explicit `vb.` entries with empty bodies — source-truth, not extractable binyan content (see Flag 2 routing below)
- No measurement path produces more than ~1,471 verbs from this source binary

Counter-evidence required to refute: would need a third independent method finding ≥1,500 binyan-bearing entries. Verifier-4 found 1,458; Builder found 1,453. Convergence is tight. Revision is binary-evidence justified.

**None-grammar target: <10% → <55% structural floor**

Evidence presented:
- Builder binary scan: None-grammar entries lack POS label AND lack binyan markers in the HALOT source binary
- Verifier-4's 10-sample categorization: 4 derived-nouns + 6 proper-names + 0 unrecognized-pattern
- 0 of 10 samples fall into the "unrecognized-pattern" category (the only category suggesting a fixable mechanical defect)
- The 50% rate reflects HALOT's editorial reality: many entries are derived nouns and proper names without POS markers

Counter-evidence required to refute: would need >2 of 10 None-grammar samples to be unrecognized-patterns (entries with body content that looks like a POS marker but the parser missed). Verifier-4 found 0. Revision is categorization-evidence justified.

### Verdict: CALIBRATION ACCEPTED

- Verb target revised to **≥1,450** with binary-evidence justification (two independent measurements converged at ~1,453–1,458 ceiling; Builder achieved 1,471 = PASS).
- None-grammar target revised to **<55% structural floor** with categorization-evidence justification (0/10 unrecognized-pattern samples; 50% is HALOT's structural reality = PASS).
- **Phase 2 final verdict: PASS.**

The "FAIL" in Verifier-4's initial framing was a calibration error in r5's aspirational targets, not a fix-quality issue. Builder's work is substantively correct.
